About The Calendars
☪️
The Hijri Calendar
The Islamic lunar calendar (AH) begins from the Hijra in 622 CE. Each year has 354 or 355 days across 12 lunar months.
☀️
The Gregorian Calendar
The world's most widely used civil calendar. It is a solar calendar of 365 days, with a leap year every 4 years.
🌙
Islamic Months
Key months include Ramadan (fasting), Dhu al-Hijjah (Hajj), Muharram (Islamic New Year) and Rabi' al-Awwal.
🔢
Year Difference
The Hijri year is about 11 days shorter than the Gregorian year. Currently ~1447 AH ≈ ~2026 CE.
