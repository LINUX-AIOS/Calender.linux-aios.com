About The Calendars
☪️
The Hijri Calendar
The Islamic lunar calendar (AH) begins from the Hijra of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in 622 CE. Each year has 354–355 days across 12 lunar months.
☀️
The Gregorian Calendar
The world's most widely used civil calendar introduced in 1582. A solar calendar of 365 days with a leap year every 4 years.
🌙
Islamic Months
Key months include Ramadan (fasting), Dhu al-Hijjah (Hajj & Eid), Muharram (New Year) and Rabi' al-Awwal (Prophet's ﷺ birthday).
🔢
Year Difference
The Hijri year is ~11 days shorter than the Gregorian year. Currently 1447 AH ≈ 2025–2026 CE.
